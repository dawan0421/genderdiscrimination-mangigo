According to the UN Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Discrimination Against Women (CEDAW), gender discrimination involves any distinction, exclusion, or restriction made on the basis of sex, with the aim of impairing women's enjoyment of human rights on equal footing with men. This discrimination can manifest in various forms, such as unequal pay, restricted access to education and jobs, and limited healthcare services.
