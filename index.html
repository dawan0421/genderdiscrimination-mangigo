WHAT IS GENDER DISCRIMINATION?



Gender discrimination is the unfair treatment of individuals based on their gender identity or expression.



Gender discrimination is not just a women's issue; it affects everyone. It deprives individuals of their basic human rights and opportunities solely based on their gender. By advocating against gender discrimination, we strive for a world where everyone, regardless of gender, has equal access to education, employment, and representation in all aspects of life. Let's work together to dismantle harmful stereotypes and create a society where everyone can thrive regardless of their gender.
